ABOUT OUR PROGRAMME

Were we meet the waves!

Hank Flavin is a former USA junior surf champion. Following a visit to Ireland in 2015 Hank fell for the waves of the coast of Sligo. Hank returned to Ireland in 2016 where he established the Easkey Surf School. Easkey Surf School has expanded significantly in recent years. While the school caters for all grades of surfers, it is Hanks significant experience as a championship surfer which is attracting students.Hank has been quoted as saying “The increased interest in surfing in Ireland, coupled with an unbelievable coast line provides huge commercial opportunities along the Atlantic Way.”


In the early years Hank was a one man operation, running the hostel and providing the surf lessons. As the business expanded Hank employed additional staff both for the provision of lessons and providing accommodation. Hank’s experience has a championship surf instructor has become well known. Following the 2015 summer season Hank has decided to redesign the surf school offerings. Students have the option of purchasing a “Surf and Stay” package which entitles the student to a 15% discount on surf lessons. The daily package is available to all surfer levels.
