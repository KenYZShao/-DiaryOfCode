About The Staff Portal

This page is for staff of Easkey surf school. It allows staff to manage books for surf lessons and accommodation.

In addition, it allows staff to check in and check out guests as they arrive onsite.

Staff will have access to all areas of the system, and will be permitted to change bills, orders, totals, and charges.

We want our guests to have the best time with us and we believe that the process is down to our highly trained and supported staff members.

We prioritise safety, so each month we take two days to do staff training, on topics like CPR, water saftey, etc.

If you need any support, please let me know, Hank Flavin.

Opening hours: From April - October Tuesday - Sunday from 9am to 5pm.

Staff Training days: On the 22nd -23rd of each month from 11am to 5pm.

Address: Surfers Road, Easkey, Co. Sligo.
